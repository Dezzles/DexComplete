What is DexComplete

DexComplete is a website designed to help you complete all of your in game Pokemon collections!
Want to track which Pokemon you have in your Pokedex? Got it. Want to track which TMs you have? That too

What can I track in my Pokemon games?

You can track the following items, each of them will be specific to the game you're playing (none of them pesky 6th gen berries in your 3rd gen lists!):
  • Pokemon (all games)
  • Hidden Abilities (Gen 5+)
  • Berries (Gen 3+)
  • Technical Machines (Gen 5+)
  • Ditto Natures
  • Egg Groups (for your max stats)
  • AND MORE TO COME (probably)
You can check out my progress in my main game by clicking right here!

How do I even?

Well first up create an account (do that in the top right hand corner), that'll take you to your player dashboard. From there you can create a game and you'll have all the tracking stuff available to you!
Once you go to say your pokedex, just click or tap on one of these and it'll cycle through these or sometimes just the first two, depending on the particular collection.
How you use these is entirely up to you, BUT the greyed out Pokeball is treated as something you don't have by the website's stats.
